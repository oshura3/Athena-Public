## 1. The 17-Step "Soulful" Narrative Filter (Reddit)

The primary insight is shifting from "SEO Content" to "Narrative Assets."

* **Angle Definition**: Before writing, list your specific angle to ensure it isn't just a re-iteration of the top 10 search results.
* **The Hidden Gem Check**: Every section must contain a "hidden gem"â€”expert-level insight that only someone who has done the work would know.
* **Tone Reference**: Keeping a "Tone North Star" (e.g., Marketermilk) open during the entire writing process to prevent tone-drift.
* **The 3-Sentence Rule**: Paragraphs must be $\le 3$ sentences. This is the "mobile readability limit."
* **Next Steps > Conclusion**: Modern readers don't want a summary of what they just read; they want a jumping-off point for action.

## 3. Atmospheric Writing Physics (Nour Eldin)

Blogging is an endurance sport. The "Cyborg" approach involves environmental priming:

1. **Environment Sync**: Designated writing area, instrumental music, and specific hydration (tea over coffee to avoid the "jitter-crash").
2. **The Vomit Draft**: Permissions to write "truly terrible" prose to bypass the self-editing bottleneck.
3. **Sprint Interval**: 30â€“45 minute focused sprints followed by a 15-minute complete head-clear.
4. **Half-Written Sentences**: Leaving a sentence unfinished when moving sections to make re-entry easier (Zeigarnik Effect).
