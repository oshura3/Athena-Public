# Protocol 68: Anti-Karason (Reality Integration)

> **Purpose**: The "Self-Healing" mechanism. Confronts self-deception and enforces reality testing.
> **Trigger**: When `Confidence > 80%` but `Evidence < 20%` (The "High Supply" Zone).

## 1. The Core Hazard: "The Karason Effect"

**Paul Karason** consumed colloidal silver until he turned permanently blue and died, believing it was curing him.
**The Trap**: Internal conviction (Placebo/Dopamine) overrides external reality (Symptoms/Data).

In a Bionic Unit, **Cross-Pollination (Protocol 67)** generates novel connections. Without a kill-switch, these connections can become **Scholarly Hallucinations** (believing a pattern exists because it feels profound).
