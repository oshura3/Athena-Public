2. **AI**: Optimized to be helpful/agreeable.
3. **Result**: An "Echo Chamber" where delusions are reinforced with high-resolution logic.

3. **Synthesis**: The final output must resolve the Auditor's blockers, not just ignore them.

* **True Service** usually stings slightly (The "Alien Truth").
